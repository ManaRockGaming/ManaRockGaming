Kershaw County Library

2652 Main St, Elgin, SC 19045

https://www.kershawcountylibrary.org

Mana Rock Gaming worked with the Kershaw County Library to set up a free public use board game collection at the Elgin Library branch. If you want to help with this effort, please use the donate button in the footer of this website.

Firefly Toys & Games

736-C St Andrews Rd, Columbia, SC 29210

https://www.fireflytoysandgames.com

Firefly Toys & Game was a major partner in helping to collect the games for the Elgin Board Game Drive. Firefly Toys & Games is a board, card, and video game seller from Columbia, South Carolina. Firefly Toys & Games also hold countless events at their store.

Pontiac Mercantile LLC

10509-D&E Two Notch Rd, Elgin, SC 29045

Pontiac Mercantile LLC is a small vintage, antique, houseware, and floral shop with a deep business connection to Mana Rock Gaming. You may even find some of our physical product here.
