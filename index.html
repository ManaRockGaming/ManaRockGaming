Our Mission

The mission of The Mana Rock Gaming Foundation is to promote public education, creativity, and community engagement in Kershaw County and South Carolina through the joy of gathering around a table or a TV with your friends. We support educational programming, workshops, conventions, and community events that encourage learning in game design, coding, computer science, history, and related fields. Through collaborative play and shared interactive experiences, we hope to create stronger communities across South Carolina. So we hope you join us at our table.
